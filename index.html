Products

  • Ice cream

    It's natural. It's yummy. Those who have tasted it, swear by it. The unique feature of the ice creams manufactured by IceCream is that they contain no artificial flavours, no preservatives or stabilisers, only fresh fruit pulp or dry fruits.

  • Ice coffee

    We use 100% Arabica coffee from Guatemala, Honduras and Colombia. Honey processed, medium roasted. Our favorite way (and the secret technique) to drink iced coffee is to pour chilled coffee over coffee ice cubes.

  • Milkshakes

    Milkshake - a fragrant cold drink based on milk and ice cream with a delicate creamy taste and airy texture. You can add Oreos, Reese's Peanut Butter Cups, brownies, cookie dough, chocolate syrup, or fruit. Anything you like!
